ChickenCurry recipe

This easy, 30-minute chicken curry recipe produces tender chicken in a rich, savory sauce, perfect for a quick weeknight dinner. It is a flexible, Indian-inspired recipe that requires minimal prep.

Ingredients
  • Chicken: 1 lb (approx. 500g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs (best for juiciness) or breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • Oil: 1 to 2 tbsp olive oil or avocado oil
  • Aromatics: 1 large yellow onion (finely diced), 2 cloves garlic (minced), 1 tbsp fresh ginger (minced)
  • Spices: 1 tbsp curry powder (mild or hot), 1 tsp ground cumin, 1 tsp ground turmeric, 1/2 tsp garam masala (plus 1/2 tsp for finishing), 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)
  • Base: 14 oz (400g) can diced tomatoes (fire-roasted recommended), 1 can (13.5 oz / 400ml) full-fat coconut milk
  • Seasoning & Garnish: 1/2 tsp sea salt (or to taste), 1/4 tsp black pepper, 1/4 cup fresh cilantro (chopped), 1 tsp lemon juice or vinegar

Steps

  1. Saut√© Aromatics: Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the diced onions, garlic, and ginger. Cook for 4-5 minutes until the onions are soft and translucent.
  2. Bloom Spices: Add the curry powder, cumin, turmeric, 1/2 tsp of the garam masala, and cayenne pepper (if using). Stir constantly for 30-60 seconds to toast the spices and release their aroma.
  3. Build Sauce: Pour in the canned diced tomatoes and coconut milk. Stir to combine and bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat
  4. Cook Chicken: Add the cubed chicken pieces to the skillet. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Finish and Serve: Stir in the remaining 1/2 tsp of garam masala and season with salt and pepper to taste. Top with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lemon juice. Serve hot with basmati rice or naan.
